Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, sauté until soft.
  4. Add ground beef (or pork) to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, eggs, and chopped parsley.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom.
  8. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers (meat sauce, noodles, cheese mixture) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a top layer of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  11. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  13. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before serving.
